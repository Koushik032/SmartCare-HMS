Professional Notes

Use the pending queue to prioritize upcoming consultations, review symptoms early, and prepare treatment decisions before live interaction with the patient.

  • Review symptoms before consultation so you can approach each pending case with better readiness.
  • For online appointments, use the meeting link directly from the queue to avoid delays at consultation time.
  • Use the prescription action promptly after consultation to move the case toward completion smoothly.
  • Keep the pending list organized so urgent, scheduled, and follow-up cases receive timely attention.
