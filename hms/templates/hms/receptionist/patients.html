Patient Directory Guide

This guide helps receptionist users search and review patient records quickly without making the cards too crowded.

Guide

Patient cards show only the most important contact information so the directory stays clean, readable, and easy to scan during front-desk work.

  • Use the search box to find patients by name, contact number, or email quickly.
  • Email and contact are kept on one line with overflow handling for cleaner alignment.
  • Patient cards use equal height and fixed spacing so all records stay visually aligned.
  • Organization text is intentionally removed from patient cards to avoid unnecessary crowding.
