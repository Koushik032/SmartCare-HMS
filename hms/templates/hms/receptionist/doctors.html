Availability Control Guide

This guide helps receptionists manage doctor availability more clearly and avoid patient booking confusion.

Guide

Use this page to control whether a doctor should appear in the patient booking list. When a doctor is unavailable, patients should not be able to select that doctor for new appointment requests.

  • Mark a doctor unavailable if they are on leave, absent, fully booked, or not accepting new patients.
  • Mark a doctor available again when they are ready to receive patient bookings.
  • Availability status helps patients choose doctors more confidently and reduces booking mistakes.
  • Always confirm the doctor’s schedule before changing availability from the reception panel.
