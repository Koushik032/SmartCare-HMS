Setup Guidance

Why this information matters

A receptionist account is responsible for managing appointment flow, patient queue visibility, front-desk support, and organization-based coordination. Accurate setup ensures cleaner healthcare operations inside SmartCare HMS.

  • Organization Name links the receptionist with the correct hospital or service branch.
  • Contact Details help maintain professional communication and support access.
  • Username & Email create a unique digital identity for secure login and account recovery.
  • Password Setup should remain strong and confidential to protect operational access.
Professional Recommendation

Use a clear organization name, valid email address, and a strong password so the receptionist can securely manage appointments, patients, and front-desk workflow without access issues.
