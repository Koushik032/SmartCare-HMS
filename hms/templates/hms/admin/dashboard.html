Complete Administrative Overview

This dashboard works as the digital command center of SmartCare HMS. It gives a complete idea of platform users, hospital coverage, appointment workload, message activity, performance, and management tools so the admin can understand the full system immediately after entering the dashboard.

  • Use the top cards to quickly understand the number of doctors, patients, appointments, messages, hospitals and receptionists.
  • Use the donut chart and bar chart to visually compare platform usage and healthcare resources.
  • Use appointment performance cards to track completed appointments, pending workload and operational progress.
  • Use administrative tools to manage doctors, patients, receptionists and appointment records without leaving the control center.
