Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

A few important questions users may ask before submitting the contact form.

Will my message be saved?

Yes. Once the form is submitted successfully, the data is saved into the database through your Django model.

Will the data go to webhook?

Yes. After saving to the database, the same contact information is sent to your configured webhook URL.

Is this flow reliable?

The form keeps the record in your own database first, so even if webhook delivery fails, you still keep the submission.
