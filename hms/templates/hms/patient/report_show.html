Submission Process

Your uploaded report will be reviewed by your selected doctor.

  • Select the doctor related to your previous appointment or consultation.
  • Upload your previous prescription and new report file clearly.
  • Add a short note explaining your current symptoms or concern.
  • Submit the report and wait for doctor review or updated prescription.

Upload Guide

Follow these tips to make the review process easier.

  • Upload clear and readable prescription/report files.
  • Write symptoms or concerns in simple and direct wording.
  • Select the correct doctor so the report goes to the right profile.
  • Do not upload unnecessary files or unrelated documents.
