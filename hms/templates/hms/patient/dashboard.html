MediBot • Basic Digital Health Assistant

MediBot helps patients understand common symptoms, basic health concerns, and simple next-step guidance before booking a consultation. It is supportive guidance only and does not replace professional medical care.

  • Reduce unnecessary immediate doctor visits for simple concerns.
  • Understand when to monitor symptoms and when to seek consultation.
